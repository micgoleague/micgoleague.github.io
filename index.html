Rules

  1. You must play all quarters in each tournament. We play tournaments daily and you must participate in all of them.
  2. If you are not going to make a tournament please let us know at least 8 hours in advance. Do not let us know just a few hours before the tournament, because all admins could be asleep and we won't get the message.
  3. You must have a 20+ point differential (So Points you scored in all 3 quarters - Points you allowed must be >=20)
  4. When you first join the league you will be asked to submit screenshots of all your quarters for your first 5 tournaments. If your average differential is 20+ you will remain with us!
  5. You must play lineups close to your overall. So if your lineup is 92 you must play a lineup that's 91-93. If there are not enough 90s you can pick 1 lineup that's 89 but only if it's necessary.
  6. Last but not least, no bullies and no beggars are welcome. Respect each other and stay friendly.

Contact

If you are interested in joining our League or you just want to say hi you can use our private chat on the bottom right corner and a League Admin will be with you shortly.
